PDX Dev Week

July 28 - August 3

PDX Dev Week is a week long celebration of the people and companies in Portland, OR who are building the software of tomorrow. Sign-up below to stay up-to-date on all of the best community-driven meetups, hackathons, workshops, job fairs and parties going on during the week of July 28th.

If you are joining us from out of town, you'll see exactly what makes Portland, OR one of the leading tech hubs in the world.

TUIO Jam - Create Interactive Art

Saturday, August 2 11:00am – 8:00pm (demos at 6:30pm)

Instrument - 419 NE 10th Ave

We are hosting a hack day in which participants will create unique electronic artworks that are all driven from a common touch surface. So one person touching one computer will cause all artworks to react. The goal is to create a gallery showing in which all art in the gallery reacts in tandem to one viewers touches. Select art pieces created at the hack day will be exhibited at the TUIO Art Show at the Diode Gallery on August 16th.
